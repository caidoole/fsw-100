FAQ's

  • What exactly does Honey Light do?

    • We work on a national scale to help connect people and companies in need with the services available that best suit them

  • Why don't people just google search for servies?

    • While that is a viable option for some, the amount of information out there can be overwhelming and it becomes increasingly difficult for them to find local information. Even after you find the help you are seeking it can seem like it just isn't the right fit. We facilitate a foundation connection that lets you get to know a company before you choose to work with them.

  • Do you accept insurance?

    • While we do connect some with health services, we do not actually provide any. This makes us ineligible to accept health insurance at this time.

  • What is Contact Comforts?

    • Contact Comfort is a purely platonic service provided by companies for those whose lives are lacking in touch. Touch is a vital aspect missing in millions of peoples lives, yet its essential for the health and happiness of individuals.
