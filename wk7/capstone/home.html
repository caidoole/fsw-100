Welcome to Honey Light Wellness, a place where those seeking support can connect with those waiting to help. We all have moments in life were things are just a little more than we can carry alone. While there is no shame in asking for help, we recognize it is still the hardest thing in life to do. Be it pride or family pressure that created a barrier between you and the help you need, we offer you the anonymity to begin exploring your options.

We offer an extensive list of services including:

  • Individual Sessions
  • Couples Counseling
  • Family Healing
  • Group Therapeutics
  • Business Strengthening
  • Contact Comforts

We work with local companies to make in-person, as well as hosting online interactions, easier; allowing you to connect from the comfort of your home till you are ready to take the next step towards the life you want to live. Don't have a local meeting place or ablility to get to one? We host area specific mixers online as needed. No one is ever as alone as they feel they are.
